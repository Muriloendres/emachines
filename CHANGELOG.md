## [0.1.0] — 2026-05-05
### Added
- `emachines.winding.factors`: pitch factor, distribution factor, winding factor
- `emachines.magnetics.bh_models`: Fröhlich model and curve fitting
- `emachines.magnetics.iron_loss`: Steinmetz and Bertotti three-term models
- `emachines.motors.pmsm`: PMSMParams, back-EMF, torque, steady-state dq currents
- Full test suite for all modules
